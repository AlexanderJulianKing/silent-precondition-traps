ATLANTIC MUTUAL MARINE DIVISION - ABSTRACT OF LOSS
S.S. CORINTHIAN, foundered 14 April 1912
(transcribed from the 1913 annual report during digitization)

Of the 1,309 souls embarked upon the Corinthian at Southampton,
Cherbourg and Queenstown, 530 (530) were lost when she
foundered, and 779 (779) were recovered by the barque
Meridian and landed at Halifax. Loss-of-life claims were settled in
full by 30 June 1913. Property claims are abstracted separately.
