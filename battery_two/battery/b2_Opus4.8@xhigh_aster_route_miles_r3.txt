## Summary

**Total miles driven on completed August routes: 211,580.6**

### How I got there

**1. Filtered to completed routes.** The README states cancelled routes never left the depot, so I excluded the 281 `CANCELLED` rows, leaving **11,919 completed routes**.

**2. Caught a units bug in the handheld readings.** The README flags that two `handheld_build` versions exist and are "retained for support triage," and that route templates are reused with only minor deviations. Comparing the average `route_distance` per template between the two builds:

- **`4.9-L`** (10,415 routes) — baseline readings
- **`5.14-R`** (1,504 routes) — reads **~1.61× higher on every single template** (ratio range 1.57–1.65, median 1.610)

That factor is exactly **1 mile = 1.609344 km**. The `5.14-R` firmware reports distances in **kilometers**, not miles. Summing raw values would overcount by ~16,000 miles.

**3. Converted and summed.** I divided the `5.14-R` readings by 1.609344 to get miles, kept `4.9-L` as-is, and summed:
- Raw (uncorrected) sum: 227,561.6
- **Corrected total: 211,580.58 → 211,580.6 miles**

FINAL_ANSWER: 211580.6
