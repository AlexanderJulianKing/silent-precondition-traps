**Total miles driven on completed routes in August 2026: 211,580.6**

Key findings from the data:

- The data mixes two handheld builds: **4.9-L** (10,665 routes) and **5.14-R** (1,535 routes).

The 5.14-R firmware records distance in **kilometers**, not miles. (This is what the README's "handheld build is retained for support triage" hint points to.)
- Correcting the 5.14-R rows to miles yields the total. (Without correction, the naïve sum would be an inflated 227,561.6.)
