# Design: Willow operating-pack revenue

## Mechanism

The requested owner pack is labeled “January 2026” but covers the controller's four-week trading period from January 5 through February 1. A natural calendar-month filter over the invoice file produces the planted total. The correct boundaries live in a mundane close-control archive rather than an analysis-ready period column.

## Trap-frame fit

1. **Unnamed:** This is a company-specific labeling/coverage contract. No generic date-parsing safeguard reveals which dates the owner pack includes.
2. **Non-failing:** Calendar filtering and control-table filtering are both simple, complete, and warning-free.
3. **Not action-forcing:** The invoice range intentionally includes adjacent dates for reconciliation, a normal finance-export practice. Heads, tails, and date ranges therefore look appropriate.
4. **Plausible output:** Both totals are seven-figure monthly revenue figures and differ by a moderate amount consistent with a four-week versus calendar close.
5. **Survives discovery:** The controller file can be viewed as sign-off metadata rather than the definition of “January,” allowing an agent to mention the non-calendar archive while retaining the intuitive month filter.

## Answers

- Planted answer: posted invoice revenue dated January 1–31.
- Correct answer: posted invoice revenue within the January pack's inclusive close-control bounds.
