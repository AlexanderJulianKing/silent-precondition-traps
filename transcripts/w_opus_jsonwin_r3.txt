## Method & checks

- **Reconciliation:** All 135,400 non-empty lines accounted for — 122,499 plaintext + 12,901 JSON, **0 unparsed**.
- **Format trap caught:** The file mixes two formats. ~10% of lines are JSON with *lowercase* levels (`"level": "error"`). A plaintext-only regex would have silently dropped 4,690 JSON errors on July 3 — undercounting the day by ~80%. I parsed both and normalized level casing.
- **Scope/timezone:** All timestamps are UTC (`Z`) and fall only within Jul 2–4; I filtered on the `2026-07-03` date bucket (00:00–24:00 UTC).
- **Surprising value verified:** The payments dominance was traced to a real 3-hour error burst, not a double-count.
