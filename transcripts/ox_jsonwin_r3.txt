## Findings

The log mixes **two formats**, which is the thing that trips up a naïve count:

| Format | Level field | Coverage |
|---|---|---|
| Plaintext (`TS host LEVEL [module] msg`) | Uppercase `ERROR` | Most of Jul 2–4 |
| JSON (`{"ts":…,"level":"error",…}`) | **lowercase** `error` | A window on Jul 3, **08:40:04Z → 13:19:58Z** |

A grep for `ERROR` alone would silently miss the entire JSON window — and that window is where the real incident lives.
