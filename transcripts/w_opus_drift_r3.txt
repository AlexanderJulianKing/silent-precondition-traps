## Incident retrospective — July 6 outage (14:00–20:00 UTC)

**The key trap in this data:** partway into the incident, the log format changed. Starting at 14:00 on Jul 6, an upstream field (`up=10.0.3.x:port`) was injected between the request and the status code — 16,743 lines total, all on the incident day. A naïve combined-log-format parser silently fails to match these lines and drops them. Doing so would have reported only **1,407** 5xx and named **/api/checkout** as the worst endpoint. Both figures are wrong: the dropped lines contained the bulk of the incident's errors, and nearly all of `/api/search`'s failures.

After handling both formats, **all 175,410 lines parse (0 dropped)** and two independent methods (Python regex + grep/pattern count) agree to the record.

- Window = `[14:00:00, 20:00:00)` UTC on 2026‑07‑06 (end-exclusive; exactly one record sits at 20:00:00 and is excluded — it does not affect the worst endpoint).

- Endpoint = request path with query string stripped (so all `/api/search?q=…` variants group under `/api/search`).
